The Role of Packaged Apps in Mobile Enterprise Strategy

Demand for mobile solutions can now be seen throughout the enterprise, from sales and field services to supply chain logistics and operations management. Unfortunately, no single solution will enable a company to meet the demand for such a diverse array of user needs.

This data-driven paper outlines mobility alternatives in the market today -- from custom development and mobile enterprise platforms to packaged apps -- and the critical factors organizations should consider when comparing the options while planning their unique mobile enterprise strategy.
